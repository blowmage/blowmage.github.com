Now that I'm blogging again, and I've had a resurgence of .NET related posts, I thought I'd add to a meme I saw going around earlier this summer. First John Lam posted his [Vibrant Ink](http://www.iunknown.com/2007/06/vibrant_ink_vis.html) theme for Visual Studio, then I saw Tomas Restrepo post his [Nightingale](http://www.winterdom.com/weblog/2007/09/11/NightingaleAVS2005ColorScheme.aspx) theme.

I've always been a fan of John Long's [Ruby Blue TextMate](http://wiseheartdesign.com/2006/3/11/ruby-blue-textmate-theme/) theme. So I created my own Visual Studio theme (including the Monaco font) a long time ago and thought I should [share it here](http://github.com/downloads/blowmage/rubyblue/RubyBlueVS2005.zip). Feel free to fork [my repo](http://github.com/blowmage/rubyblue), improve it, and send me a pull request. Enjoy.
